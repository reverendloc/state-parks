### GOLD COUNTRY NORTH LOOP
**Duration: 4 days | Parks: 6 | ~500 miles total**
**Suggested Dates: 4th of July Weekend — Fri–Mon, Jul 3–6, 2026**

The full story of California's gold rush. Empire Mine was the largest, deepest mine in CA history. Malakoff Diggins shows the catastrophic scale of hydraulic mining — and on Saturdays you get the guided ghost-town tour *and* gold panning. Marshall Gold Discovery is where it all started in 1848. Indian Grinding Rock preserves 1,185 Miwok bedrock mortars.

> **Why 4 days?** Santa Rosa → Empire Mine is a solid 2h40m drive (~3 hrs padded), so a 9:00 AM departure reaches Grass Valley around lunch. Spreading the parks over four days keeps every day relaxed and lets Day 2 capture Malakoff's full Saturday schedule (1:30pm tour + 3pm gold panning).

## PART 5: PRACTICAL TIPS

### Before You Go
- **Verify hours**: Many historic parks have limited days/hours (often Thu–Sun or Fri–Mon). Check [parks.ca.gov](https://www.parks.ca.gov) before each trip.
- **Folsom Powerhouse** tours run weekends only — plan accordingly.
- **Antelope Valley Indian Museum**: Sat/Sun only, 11am–4pm. The SoCal trip is built around this.
- **Bodie SHP**: Open May–October only. Last 3 mi unpaved but usually fine for regular vehicles in summer. Bring water — no services.
- **Malakoff Diggins**: There IS an all-paved route — 26 mi / ~50 min from Nevada City via Tyler Foote Rd → Cruzon Grade → Back Bone Rd → Derbec Rd. (Ignore older "dirt road only" warnings.)
- **Robert Louis Stevenson SHP**: Monument is a short walk; the summit hike is 10+ mi and strenuous. No dogs.
- **Bidwell Mansion**: Closed since the Dec 2024 fire — verify reopening before Road Trip to Chico.
- **Will Rogers SHP**: Ranch house/stables destroyed in the Jan 2025 Palisades Wildfire; park reopened with limited access — verify status.

### Booking Campgrounds
